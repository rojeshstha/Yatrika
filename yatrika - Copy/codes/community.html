Welcome to the Yatrika Community

Yatrika is more than just a trek planning platform — it’s a growing community of adventurers, local partners, and experienced guides working together to make trekking in Nepal accessible, affordable, and authentic. Our mission is to connect travelers with the true heart of Nepal by offering everything from affordable homestays and quality trekking gear to group treks and trusted local guides — all in one place.

At the core of Yatrika are the people: passionate trekkers, experienced professionals, and first-time adventurers who believe in meaningful, budget-friendly travel. For local businesses and trekking agencies, Yatrika provides a free platform to promote their services, reach a wider audience, and become a recognized part of the trekking network. Whether you're exploring the trails or offering services to those who do, Yatrika is here to support your journey and foster a community built on trust, experience, and shared love for the mountains.

WRITTEN ON 6 March, 2025

Exploring the Manaslu Circuit – A Hidden Gem Made Accessible by Yatrika :

WRITTEN ON 30 April, 2003

As someone who loves off-the-beaten-path adventures, the Manaslu Circuit had always been on my list. But the remote nature of the trek and lack of clear planning resources made it seem difficult to organize on my own. That changed when I came across Yatrika. From day one, the platform guided me through everything — selecting the best time to go, finding affordable homestays along the route, and even comparing gear rental prices. I also connected with experienced trekkers through Yatrika’s community, who shared tips that made a huge difference. What felt impossible before became one of the best experiences of my life — all thanks to how Yatrika connects travelers with local expertise.
