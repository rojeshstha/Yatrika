Yatrika

Yatrika is a trek planning platform built to connect trekkers with the real heart of Nepal. We help travelers find affordable homestays, rent or buy trekking gear, join group treks, and book trusted local guides and agencies — all in one place. Our goal is to make trekking simpler, more budget-friendly, and more meaningful.

For local businesses and agencies, Yatrika offers a free platform to showcase services, reach more customers, and grow their presence in the trekking community. Whether you're planning a solo adventure or offering a service, Yatrika is your partner on the journey.

Where Every Trek Begins

Connecs with local guides, homestays, gear, and real experiences across Nepal to help you plan smarter, spend less, and explore deeper.

Responsible Tourism

Every time you do a hiking trip with us you’re helping to conserve wild landscapes for our kids and for future generations.

Support Local Communities

Yatrika is a purely nurtured and owned native trekking company in Nepal.
