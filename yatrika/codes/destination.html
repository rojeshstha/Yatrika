Nepal is a place where each corner speaks a story be it the mighty Himalayas, the green hills, serene villages, and old cities. It is a destination for dreamers and adventurers alike, where one can have an intimate experience of nature, culture, and adventure. Whether one is pursuing sunrises over snow-capped mountains or walking through age-old temples, each location here has something unique to offer.

For trekkers, nothing compares to Nepal. Tread legendary routes like Everest Base Camp, Annapurna Circuit, or serene trails of Langtang and Manaslu. Prefer solitude? Off-beat routes take you over virgin landscapes and friendly homestays, where your relationship with nature and local culture is more intimate.

Even if you have just one day, Nepal is never short of activities. Make short treks within and around Kathmandu or Pokhara, go for cultural tours, rafting, or spiritual sites and hidden villages. Whether it's a weekend getaway or a once-in-a-lifetime trek, our personally selected destinations help you plan every aspect of the trip.
